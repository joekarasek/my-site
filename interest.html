Nature

I am an avid outdoorsman and environmentalist. In my previous career, I worked as a field botanist on several environmental restoration and protection projects. I care deeply about protecting the environment, especially our watersheds and natural areas. In my off-time, I enjoy hiking and skiing in the beautiful Pacific Northwest.

Music

As an amatuer musician I not only enjoy listening to music, but also playing music. I have played the piano for many years and recently purchased a melodica so I can play on the go. I enjoy all types of music, but mostly play classic rock (Beatles, Pink Floyd, etc.). If you would like to jam sometime, feel free to contact me.

Origami

Origami is a wonderful art. The goal of origami is to take a flat sheet of paper and create thought-provoking art by simply folding. No cutting, tearing, or glueing is allowed. I hope to put a gallery of my origami art up soon...

Volunteering

One of my core values is to Pay It Forward, which is why I enjoy volunteering my time for various causes. For more than three years I have lead tree planting events with community members, corporate groups, and students throughout the Portland metro area (I have help plant more than 15,000 trees). I also volunteer with Friends of Trees, another wonderful Portland-based non-profit.
